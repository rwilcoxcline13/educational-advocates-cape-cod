    Why you need an educational advocate?

    Special education is a complicated process, which is why you need a professional not only to help you in understanding the laws surrounding the entitlements your child deserves but to strongly advocate for your child. With over 30 years of in classroom experiece we are able to support and empower our clients in order to achieve the best possible learning outcomes for their children.

    Navigation

    We have the knowledge and expertise regarding special education as well as the applicable federal and state laws. These laws are often complex and difficult to understand. We are able to offer assistance in navigating:

    • Eligibiliy Determination
    • Assesment Guidance and Interpretation
    • Compliance Timelines

    Advocacy

    Our advocates provide representation and support for any children that fall under the umbrella of special education. It is imperative that you know your childs rights and that you have a professional to support you in:

    • IEP/504 writing assistance and interpretation
    • Preparation and review of documents for IEP meetings
    • Representation at hearings to ensure compliance

    Success

    Often school systems drag their feet in providing the services your child deserves. We will gather and analyze all documents relating to your child, provide data we use to optimize the learning outcomes of the student. We provide services in:

    • Analyzing classroom reports, report cards, and other documents
    • Specialist referral: tutors, reading specialists, therapists
    • Navigating the college process
